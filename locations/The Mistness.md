## About
The Mistness is a dense and opaque cloud mass, covering the northern pole of [[Albedia]]. Above these clouds lies gigantic suspended rocks, believed to have been torn from the planet itself, and that levitates without moving.
Under those rocks, using clever constructions attached to them, live a people called [[the SkyHangers]].
Above the rocks, breathable air become too thin to live.
