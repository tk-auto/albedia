# Elina and Lem

> **Stub.** *Elina and Lem* (working title) is a tale of
> [Albedia](../index.md), narrated in the first person by
> [Lem](../characters/lem.md) — a golem-like being that
> [Elina](../characters/elina.md) created from peat at the edge of a lake. The
> tale itself is not yet set down; so far the canon holds its annexes — the two
> characters and the account of
> [the Life Components](../myths/the-life-components.md).

## Connections

- [Lem](../characters/lem.md) — the narrator and protagonist.
- [Elina](../characters/elina.md) — his maker, whom he is driven to protect.
- [The Life Components](../myths/the-life-components.md) — the cosmology the
  tale rests on: Earth, Water, Air, Fire, Soul, and the binding Malia.
