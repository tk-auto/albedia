# Val Verde

Val Verde is a long, deep valley near the heart of [Hereterra](hereterra.md), in
[Albedia](../index.md), hemmed in by sheer cliffs and filled to their brim with
dense vegetation. The [Diamond River](diamond-river.md) spills into its northern
head, just below the crater of [Col Vulca](col-vulca.md).

## Geography

Walled on either side by steep cliffs, Val Verde runs long and deep through the
region. Growth crowds it from floor to rim — a thick green tangle packed all the
way to the top of its walls, fed by the waters of the
[Diamond River](diamond-river.md), which flows down from
[Barren Peaks](barren-peaks.md) and enters the valley at its northern
head, below [Col Vulca](col-vulca.md), before running on southward down its
length.
