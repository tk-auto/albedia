# Panklin Citadel

Panklin Citadel is a heavily fortified city in the south-east of
[Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md), whose citizens are kept
entirely cut off from the rest of the world.

## The city

Behind its weight of walls, Panklin Citadel admits nothing of the outside in and
lets nothing out. Its population is held deliberately in check: the number of
births is controlled so that the city never outgrows itself and never falls into
overcrowding.
