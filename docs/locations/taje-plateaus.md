# Taje Plateaus

The Taje Plateaus are a broken cluster of high, airy plateaus in the south of
[Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md) — exposed to the wind, yet
notably fertile.

## Geography

Fragmented into separate tablelands raised above the surrounding country, the
Taje Plateaus catch the full force of the wind. Despite that exposure, their soil
is particularly rich.

## Trade

Trade runs steadily between the Taje Plateaus and the
[Esper Counties](esper-counties.md) to the north, carried along
[Nerem's Trail](diamond-river.md#nerems-trail), which has been broadly improved
for the convoys that use it.
