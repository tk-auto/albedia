# Sea of Lysin

The Sea of Lysin is a shallow, usually very calm sea along the southern and
western edges of [Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md), heavily
travelled by merchant ships.

## Geography

Shallow and mild, the Sea of Lysin rarely turns rough, which makes it a favoured
route for trade. Its oldest harbour, [Port Terré](port-terre.md), sits on its
shore. Far out in its waters lie the [Calyps Islands](calyps-islands.md).
