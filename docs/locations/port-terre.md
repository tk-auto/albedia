# Port Terré

Port Terré is the oldest port on the [Sea of Lysin](sea-of-lysin.md), on the
south-western coast of [Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md).

## The port

The eldest harbour of the Sea of Lysin, Port Terré has stood over its calm,
shallow waters longer than any other — a natural anchor for the merchant shipping
that plies the sea.
