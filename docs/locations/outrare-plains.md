# Outrare Plains

The Outrare Plains are vast, semi-arid expanses in the west of
[Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md), roamed by nomadic peoples.

## Geography

Wide and dry, the Plains stretch across the region's western reaches, bounded by
the [Sea of Lysin](sea-of-lysin.md) to the south and the crater of
[Col Vulca](col-vulca.md) to the east. Nomad peoples move across them, following
the land rather than settling it.

## Inhabitants

The Outrare Plains are crossed by nomadic peoples.

*The nomads of Outrare are noted here but not yet recorded as a
[people](../peoples/index.md) — a natural next entry.*
