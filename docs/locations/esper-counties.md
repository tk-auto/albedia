# Esper Counties

The Esper Counties are a stretch of fertile, hilly country in the north of
[Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md) — one of the most
sought-after places in the region to make a home.

## Geography

Rolling and green, the Counties' good soil and gentle hills make them prized
land. They lie between the crater of [Col Vulca](col-vulca.md) to the west and
the waters of the [Diamond River](diamond-river.md) to the east.

## Trade

Trade runs steadily between the Esper Counties and the
[Taje Plateaus](taje-plateaus.md) to the south. To carry it, the route known as
[Nerem's Trail](diamond-river.md#nerems-trail) — named for the
[Nerem](../characters/nerem.md) who first found the way — has been broadly
improved for the passage of convoys.
