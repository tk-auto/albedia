# Hereterra

Hereterra is a broad, varied region of [Albedia](../index.md) — a land of
semi-arid plains, fertile hills and plateaus, a wide branching river, and a
scoured desert, ringed by mountains and edged to the south-west by the shallow
[Sea of Lysin](sea-of-lysin.md).

*Note: the original vault spelled this both "Hereterra" and "Herreterra". This
wiki standardizes on **Hereterra**.*

## Geography

Hereterra spans a wide range of country. The semi-arid
[Outrare Plains](outrare-plains.md) roll across the north-west, giving way to the
old crater of [Col Vulca](col-vulca.md) and the fertile hills of the
[Esper Counties](esper-counties.md). Along the north-east rise
[the Barren Peaks](the-barren-peaks.md), from which the
[Diamond River](diamond-river.md) descends in a maze of branches and marsh,
running west to spill into the northern head of [Val Verde](val-verde.md), just
below [Col Vulca](col-vulca.md).

Down the region's spine runs [Val Verde](val-verde.md), a deep cliff-walled
valley choked with green, which the Diamond River enters at its northern head;
while the windswept but fertile
[Taje Plateaus](taje-plateaus.md) rise to the south. To the east, the steep
[Mount Ballor](mount-ballor.md) stands apart from the Barren Peaks, and beyond it
the corrosive [Scythed Lands](the-scythed-lands.md) waste away under a ceaseless
wind. The isolated [Panklin Citadel](panklin-citadel.md) guards the south-east,
while the ancient [Port Terré](port-terre.md) anchors trade on the
[Sea of Lysin](sea-of-lysin.md).

## Places of Hereterra

| Place | What it is |
| --- | --- |
| **[Panklin Citadel](panklin-citadel.md)** | A heavily fortified city whose people are kept wholly isolated. |
| **[Col Vulca](col-vulca.md)** | The crater of a long-dead volcano, its inner slopes bristling with basalt needles. |
| **[Esper Counties](esper-counties.md)** | Fertile, rolling hill-country and a much-prized place to live. |
| **[Diamond River](diamond-river.md)** | A wide, branching river of marshes, rising in the Barren Peaks. |
| **[Sea of Lysin](sea-of-lysin.md)** | A shallow, calm sea busy with merchant shipping. |
| **[Mount Ballor](mount-ballor.md)** | A sheer peak apart from the Barren Peaks, holding the temple of the Sleeping God. |
| **[The Barren Peaks](the-barren-peaks.md)** | A mid-altitude range roamed by ferocious beasts. |
| **[Outrare Plains](outrare-plains.md)** | Vast semi-arid steppe crossed by nomad peoples. |
| **[Taje Plateaus](taje-plateaus.md)** | A fractured cluster of airy, wind-scoured, fertile plateaus. |
| **[Port Terré](port-terre.md)** | The oldest port on the Sea of Lysin. |
| **[The Scythed Lands](the-scythed-lands.md)** | A desert swept by a continuous corrosive wind. |
| **[Val Verde](val-verde.md)** | A long, deep valley walled by cliffs and filled with dense growth. |
