# Col Vulca

Col Vulca is the crater of a very old, long-extinct volcano in the north of
[Hereterra](hereterra.md), in [Albedia](../index.md).

## Geography

The volcano is dead, but its cauldron endures. The inner flanks of the crater are
sheathed in sharp needles of basalt, a bristling slope of black stone left by the
mountain's cooling.
