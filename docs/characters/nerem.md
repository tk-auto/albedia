# Nerem

> **Stub.** Nerem is the one who first found the overland route between the
> [Esper Counties](../locations/esper-counties.md) and the
> [Taje Plateaus](../locations/taje-plateaus.md), in [Hereterra](../locations/hereterra.md).
> The way was named **Nerem's Trail** in their honour and later improved for
> trade convoys. Nothing else is recorded yet — tell Claude who Nerem was.
