# Lem

Lem is a made being of [Albedia](../index.md) — the equivalent of a golem,
quickened from the peat of a lakeshore along the
[Diamond River](../locations/diamond-river.md), in
[Hereterra](../locations/hereterra.md), when [Elina](elina.md) unknowingly
injected life into it. He narrates the tale of
[Elina and Lem](../stories/elina-and-lem.md) in the first person.

## Overview

Lem began as a bed of peat at the edge of a lake along the Diamond River. *The
lake itself is not yet named.* Elina, who carries an affinity for
[Malia](../myths/the-life-components.md#malia) she is unaware of, injected
life into the peat without knowing what she was doing, and Lem rose from it.
Neither of them knows the truth of his origin.

*Authorial note: in any telling of the tale, Lem's nature as a made being and
Elina's unknowing authorship of him are withheld — hinted at through golem
mythology, but never revealed. No character in the world knows it, Elina and
Lem included.*

## Malia and the Life Components

Lem cannot use Malia himself. His nature gives him instead a strong tie to the
[Life Components](../myths/the-life-components.md) — the six elements whose
interaction composes life — and through that tie he can interact with the way
others wield Malia, even though the power itself is closed to him.

## Bond with Elina

Lem is bound to his maker in body as well as heart: when Elina is far from him
he suffers physically. He feels something paternal toward her, and protects
her systematically, with no regard for his own life. He does not know where
the bond comes from.
