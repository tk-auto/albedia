# Characters

The named individuals of [Albedia](../index.md) — the people and beings who move
its history.

- **[Elina](elina.md)** — a young woman of the Taje Plateaus, unknowing
  wielder of Malia and creator of Lem.
- **[Lem](lem.md)** — a golem-like being quickened from peat by the Diamond
  River, narrator of [Elina and Lem](../stories/elina-and-lem.md).
- **[Nerem](nerem.md)** — who first found the trail through Hereterra that now
  bears their name. *Stub.*
