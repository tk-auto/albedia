# Elina

Elina is a young adult of [Albedia](../index.md), raised on the
[Taje Plateaus](../locations/taje-plateaus.md) of
[Hereterra](../locations/hereterra.md), and the unknowing creator of
[Lem](lem.md). She carries an affinity for
[Malia](../myths/the-life-components.md#malia) that she is entirely unaware
of.

## Overview

Elina's mother died when she was six, and her father, always at sea, never
came back. She grew up on the windswept, fertile Taje Plateaus with that
double absence at the centre of her life.

## The making of Lem

At a lake along the [Diamond River](../locations/diamond-river.md), Elina's
unrecognized gift answered the void her lost parents had left: without knowing
what she was doing, she injected life into the peat of the lakeshore and
created [Lem](lem.md). She does not know the act was hers, and does not know
what Lem is.

*Authorial note: in any telling of the tale, Lem's nature as a made being and
Elina's unknowing authorship of him are withheld — hinted at through golem
mythology, but never revealed. No character in the world knows it, Elina and
Lem included.*
