# Elina

> **Stub.** Elina is a wielder of
> [Malia](../myths/the-life-components.md#malia) in [Albedia](../index.md),
> known so far as the creator of [Lem](lem.md) — she shaped him from peat at
> the edge of a lake and injected life into him. Little else is recorded yet —
> tell Claude who Elina is, and this will grow.
