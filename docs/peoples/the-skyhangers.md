# The SkyHangers

The SkyHangers are a people of the [Mistness](../locations/mistness.md), the
clouded northern pole of [Albedia](../index.md). They live beneath the great
floating **Rocs**, dwelling in clever constructions attached to the undersides of
the stone — hung between the impassable cloud below and the airless heights
above.

Their known settlements are the cities of [Panacea](../locations/panacea.md) and
[Calduca](../locations/calduca.md).

## Way of life

The world of the SkyHangers shows no ground at all — only a sheet of cloud
stretching away to the horizon. Nor can anyone live *above* a Roc, where the air
runs too thin to breathe. And so the SkyHangers make their home on the underside
of the stone, their whole civilization suspended beneath it.

Their cities are raised like hanging scaffolding, fixed to one or more Rocs by
**anchors** and reaching down into the open air below. Within a city, agile
**coursers** carry goods from place to place, vaulting and running through the
lattice of struts and platforms. Between cities — for the Rocs stand apart, and
the cloud below cannot be crossed — travel is by **glider**, the limited-range
flying wings that lend both the craft and its pilot their name.

## Homeland & settlements

The SkyHangers dwell in the [Mistness](../locations/mistness.md), beneath its
motionless Rocs:

- **[Panacea](../locations/panacea.md)** — a giant city straddling the undersides
  of three Rocs: the Derrite, the Sigma-1, and the TX-P.
- **[Calduca](../locations/calduca.md)** — a medium-sized city beneath the
  Calduca Roc.

## Terminology

Terms particular to the world of the SkyHangers, also collected in the world
[Glossary](../glossary.md).

Anchor
:   A fixing point where a city's structures are made fast to the underside of a
    Roc, tended and guarded by dedicated teams. Because the stone cannot be
    pierced, anchors are cunningly set into the Roc's natural irregularities; and
    because the suspended weight shifts as construction is added or removed, the
    anchoring must be constantly readjusted to keep the load in balance.

Courser
:   A courier who carries a delivery from one point to another — a trade for the
    nimble, who move by running and vaulting through the structures of the city.

Glider
:   Both the limited-range flying wing used to travel between cities and the
    pilot who flies it.

Roc
:   One of the great floating masses of stone from which the SkyHangers' cities
    hang. A Roc is fixed and motionless; its stone cannot be pierced and does not
    erode. The known Rocs are listed on
    the [Mistness](../locations/mistness.md) page.
